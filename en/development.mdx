<Info>
  **Prerequisite**: You need to have created your chatbot.
</Info>

Learn how to configure and personalize your chatbot's behavior and appearance.

<Tabs>

  <Tab title = "Behavior and context">
    **Business Context:** You can provide additional context of your business so the AI can have more information.

    

     **Custom Prompt:** The tool is based on the text provided by the user. Therefore, it is recommended to provide a clear and detailed description so the bot can function optimally. 

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/contexto.png" alt="Business Context" />
    </Frame>

    <Tip>
      Mention what the bot's objectives are, what its personality should be like, or even configure context so your chatbot understands and responds better to user queries.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title = "Custom messages">
    **Start Message**: Configure the initial message that your chatbot will display when users start a conversation.

    **Custom message**: Allows us to give the user a predefined message to start the conversation with the bot.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/parametros/en/customMessage.png" alt="Welcome message" />
    </Frame>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title = "Breakpoints">

    **Breakpoints**: "Breakpoints" are words that allow us to control the update or restart of key points of the bot, use them to restart context of conversations

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/parametros/en/breakpoints.png" alt="Welcome message" />
    </Frame>

  </Tab>


</Tabs>

<Accordion title="Best Practices">
- Start Message: Keep it concise and friendly to set the right tone for conversations
- Custom Instructions: Be specific about your chatbot's role and expertise to improve response accuracy
- Knowledge Base: Update your information sources regularly to ensure accurate and current information
-Branding: Maintain consistency with your brand's visual identity

</Accordion>

## How to create your chatbot?
<Steps>
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
   Click on the 'Create Chatbot' button located in the upper right corner of your dashboard.
   <Frame>
    <img src="/images/dashboard-en.png" alt="Dashboard" />
   </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Talk to the chatbot creation assistant">
    Interact with our intelligent assistant that will guide you through the creation process.
   <Frame>
    <img src="/images/crear-chatbot-1.png" alt="Creation assistant" />
   </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your business">
    Tell the assistant about your business so it can configure your chatbot appropriately.
   <Frame>
    <img src="/images/crear-chatbot-2.png" alt="Describe your business" />
   </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Give your chatbot a name">
    Choose a unique and representative name for your chatbot.
   <Frame>
    <img src="/images/crear-chatbot-3.png" alt="Chatbot name" />
   </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test your bot">
    Test your chatbot to make sure it works correctly.
   <Frame>
    <img src="/images/crear-chatbot-4.png" alt="Test your bot" />
   </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your chatbot">
    Save the configuration and your chatbot will be ready to use.
   <Frame>
    <img src="/images/crear-chatbot-5.png" alt="Save chatbot" />
   </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
